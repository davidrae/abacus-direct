Local

The most common type of Abacus system is the fully integrated, local installation. This is a complete, in house package that is built and installed on the premises.

All the data is collected and processed on site, and the system can be accessed and used internally. All devices connect to the system through and internal network, that can be completely seperate from other networks already in place.

The system has the ability to be accessed externally as well. If the measures are available and in place, Abacus can be viewed off site through a VPN or domain name host.
