Retail

Why is Abacus a neccessity for modern-day retail? Abacus can take in thousands of counts from cameras providing fast and fluid feedback which can be easily decrypted through multiple easy to use and easily understandable charts and graphs. Abacus will allow managers to view the busiest days, locations, most populated areas and ratios such as number of customers to number of customers having purchased an item.

Abacus has an almost unlimited range of uses, it can be used in every situation. For example if wanting to set up a vendor or stall, Abacus can accurately tell you which camera is picking up the most counts, which location is the highest populated at any given time, on any given day 24/7.

Weekly, monthly or annual reports can be automated so you are always kept up-to-date with what's going on. Not only is Abacus a tool in safety and security but can also be indirectly cost effective, via Abacus you are able to easily view which days of the week are the busiest through the counts system, after learning this you could employ more people on certain days and less on other days, depending on the number of customers you're receiving.

Abacus will provide you with everything you need to know in modern-day retail, ensuring you are kept on top of everything going on and are able to stay ahead of the game with an intricate yet easy to use counting system. Which will not only enhance both security and safety but marketing and advertising techniques and strategies.
