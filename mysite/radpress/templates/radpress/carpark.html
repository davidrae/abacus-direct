Car Parks

Car Parks may require counting applications for a number of different reasons.

You may want to calculate the average stay time of a car, or find out who is frequently staying too long.

You may want to know how full the car park is at any given time, so you can display the capacity on a variable message sign. This can help direct traffic to other car parks, or to less full areas.

Whatever the reason for collecting data on cars entering a car park, Abacus can provide you with the data you need.

Abacus can integrate with a multitude of devices to help deliver accurate data. Smart devices such as ANPR cameras, Analytics cameras, barriers, infrared trip wires and pressure sensors can all be used to generate metadata which Abacus can then use as meaningful counts.
