Live Alerts

Abacus can produce live alerts based on parameters created by the user. They can receive a plethora of alerts via email or SMS. Abacus also performs routine diagnostics on all external devices, to ensure all system components are working as intended.

  • Be notified of capacity levels in your establishment, and always be informed with up to date information. Or receive warnings when certain thresholds are met.

  • Notifications can be sent about the system diagnostics. Whether devices have been disconnected, or if they are not working properly, you can find out instantly.
